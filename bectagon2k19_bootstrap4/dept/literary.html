  • Each participating team may comprise of maximum 2 members (Min 1).

  • The abstract should be in .doc or .pdf format or .ppt format only.
  • The font of the abstract should be “Times New Roman” font of 12 font size only and for specific keywords use font size 14 (Bold). Line spacing should be 1.5.
